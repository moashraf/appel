The iPhone 8 Plus hit the market on September 22, 2017 alongside the iPhone X and its smaller counterpart, the iPhone 8. While aesthetically very similar to its predecessor, the iPhone 7 Plus, the 8 Plus saw the addition of a stainless-steel-and-glass form factor (allowing the use of wireless charging), a faster processor, improved cameras, and an improved display.

Camera improvements included the ability to capture 4K video at 24, 30, and 60 frames per second, as well as 1080p video at 30, 60, 120, and 240 frames per second. The display, the same seen in the iPhone 7 series, now features True Tone technology, which automatically adjusts the screen based on surrounding ambient lighting.

Despite these leaps in iPhone evolution, however, Apple has yet to perfect invincibility. It's sad, but your iPhone 8 Plus is still vulnerable to drops, cracks, and breakage of all kinds. iPhone 8 Plus Screen Repairs & Fixes

 

iPhone 8 Plus Screen Repair

That enormous 5.5-inch display means more room for cracks and other damage. If your iPhone 8 Plus's screen now resembles a cobweb, it's time for you to get a replacement display. Contact us and we'll set you up with a repair at any time and any place you choose!

Charging Port

With the addition of wireless charging capabilities, your charging port isn't the only way to charge your iPhone 8 Plus. It is, however, the only way to listen to wired ear buds or headphones. If your charging port isn't working, check for dust or other debris and clean it out, if necessary. Still not working? Let us know.

Microphone

Are callers hearing a thick coat of static or other strange noises when on the phone with you? There's likely something wrong with your microphone. Check if dirt or dust has gotten caught in your mic and give it a quick spray of compressed air. If that doesn't do the trick, contact us and we'll set you up with an iPhone 8 Plus repair.

Ear Speaker

You didn't pay however much money you paid for your iPhone 8 Plus to not be able to make clear calls. Hearing static or fuzzy noises when on the phone? Your ear speaker probably needs to be replaced. Fortunately for you, we can handle that.

Side Button

The most common culprit behind stubborn side buttons is stickiness caused by spilled liquids like soda or juice. Give your side buttons a good clean, and if you're still having difficulty pressing them, give us a heads up. We'll send an iTech directly to you to fix your phone.

Front/Rear Camera

The iPhone 8 Plus has two state-of-the-art cameras, but they kind of go to waste if they aren't working as they should. If you're experiencing crashes every time you open your camera or your photos are coming out black or blurry, it's time for you to get your camera fixed.

Power Button

Trying and trying with all your might to press your power button, but to no avail? It might just be stuck due to dirt or grime. Give it a good clean and see if that solves the issue. If not, you're likely due for a power button repair. We can take care of it, no problem.

Loud Speaker

There are few things worse than loading up Netflix or a YouTube video on your phone and not being able to hear it--or having it covered in a blanket of static or crackling sounds. Sound familiar? You might have dirt or dust lodged inside your loud speaker, or you might just need an iPhone 8 Plus repair. We can handle the latter.
