The iPhone X (pronounced "ten") hit the market on November 3, 2017, following a long development cycle of just about five years, and brought significant internal and external changes to Apple's long-running line of smartphones. Featuring a nearly bezel-less design, a 5.8-inch OLED display, a new, stainless-steel-and-glass form factor, wireless charging capabilities, and the lack of a home button, the X completely revolutionized the iPhone.

Ditching the iconic home button also meant doing away with Apple's Touch ID fingerprint scanner and replacing it with Face ID, the company's highly-touted facial recognition system. Housed in a set of special cameras in the "notch" living at the top of the device, Face ID projects more than 30,000 infrared dots onto a user's face to create a 3D facial map, and can learn changes in a user's face over time, allowing the system to authenticate a user even when they're wearing glasses, hats, scarves, and many other accessories.

You would think such advanced technology onboard the iPhone X would mean complete invincibility is just another feature loaded onto it. Not so. Charging Port

If the charging port on your X stopped working, there are a few things you can try. You could buy a wireless charger and charge your phone that way, you could check your charging port for any dirt or debris that could be causing interference, or you could contact us to fix your phone X. Trust us: We're really good at what we do. 

Microphone

Without a fully-functional mic, your callers are going to have a really hard time hearing you clearly. Are friends and family hearing staticky, fuzzy, or any sort of strange noises when on the phone with you? Check for dust or debris that might be lodged in your microphone. If cleaning it doesn't work, let us know and we'll head to you to fix your iPhone X.

Ear Speaker

Hearing crackling or staticky sounds when on calls is not normal iPhone X behavior--or normal behavior for any phone, really. Check for lint or dust that could be causing problems and clean it out, if found. If that doesn't solve the problem, you're likely in need of an ear speaker repair job.

Side Button

Has adjusting the volume on your iPhone been troublesome or even impossible lately? Dirt or grime could be causing your side buttons to stick. If that's the case, give them a good cleaning, but if that doesn't work, give us a heads up. One of our iTechs will head to you to fix your iPhone X.

Front/Rear Camera

Is your Camera app crashing when you try opening it or are your photos coming out black or blurry? Something's definitely wrong with your camera. Let iCracked set up a repair for you so we can get your phone in working order again.

Power Button

Activating Siri and turning your phone on and off becomes pretty hard when your power button isn't functional. Spilled liquids and dirt can cause your power button to stick, so clean it off if you think that might be the problem. If it's still on the fritz, set up a repair with us and we'll have your phone working again in no time at all.

Loud Speaker

The iPhone X's stereo speakers are essentially useless if all you can hear is staticky or fuzzy sounds. Sometimes, lodged dirt or dust can be the culprit behind loud speaker issues. If spraying a few shots of compressed air to clear out your loud speaker doesn't do the trick, turn to us and we can handle your loud speaker iPhone X repair.
