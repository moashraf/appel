The iPhone 8 hit store shelves on September 22, 2017 alongside its brothers the iPhone 8 Plus and iPhone X. In terms of form factor, the 8 wasn't a significant departure from the iPhone 7 series, outwardly looking almost exactly the same apart from the addition of a glass back. With it came wireless charging capabilities, as well as a faster processor and upgraded cameras.

Although the 8 retained the Retina HD Display found in the iPhone 7, it also includes True Tone technology, which adjusts the screen automatically based on ambient lighting. Additionally, the 8 comes with a 12 MP back camera capable of shooting 4K video at 24, 30, or 60 frames per second and 1080p video at 30, 60, 120, or 240 frames per second.

With such advanced technology inside the iPhone 8, you would think total invincibility is just another feature loaded onto it. Not so. Your iPhone is still vulnerable as ever, and with a glass back, breakage is even easier. That's why iCracked specializes in iPhone 8 repairs and iPhone 8 screen repairs.

iPhone 8 Screen Repairs & Other Fixes

 

iPhone 8 Screen Repair

That gorgeous Retina HD Display is absolutely wasted if a giant crack (or multiple giant cracks, or even multiple little cracks) is running through it. If your iPhone 8 is suffering from a broken display, let us know. Our team of repair experts will come to you whenever, wherever you want and set you up with an iPhone 8 screen repair.

Charging Port

While you can still charge your iPhone 8 wirelessly if your charging port is broken, a busted port also means listening to music with wired earbuds is impossible. If your charging port isn't acting as it should, check for dirt, debris, or other obstructions and clean them out. If that doesn't work, let us know and we'll get things fixed.

Microphone

Making phone calls on a phone with a broken mic can be very frustrating, especially if callers can't understand a thing you say. If friends and family can only hear static or other strange noises when on the phone with you, try checking for dust or dirt stuck in your microphone. If cleaning it doesn't work, a hardware issue may be to blame. Contact us and we'll come save the day.

Ear Speaker

Are you hearing static, fuzzy noises, or any other sound that doesn't belong when on phone calls? Spoiler alert: you have a broken ear speaker. Hit "repair my iPhone 8" above to set up a repair. One of our all-star iTechs will meet you at a location of your choice, at a time of your choice, to fix your iPhone 8. It's that easy!

Side Button

Dealing with stubborn side buttons? Without them, you'll find adjusting the volume on your iPhone to be a bit troublesome. If pressing your side buttons is proving difficult, they might be stuck due to dirt or grime. Clean them off, and if that doesn't help, let us know. We'll get you scheduled for an iPhone 8 repair.

Front/Rear Camera

The iPhone 8 is capable of shooting 4K video at 60 frames per second, but that doesn't mean anything if your camera isn't working. Is your Camera app crashing, or are your photos turning out blurry or not at all? Sounds like you need your iPhone camera repaired. We're the right ones for the job.

Power Button

If you're finding it increasingly difficult (or even outright impossible) to turn your iPhone on and off, you might have dirt or grime causing your power button to stick. Clean the area, and if that doesn't work, contact us to set up a repair. Our technicians will meet you at home, at your place of work, or even at your favorite coffee shop to fix your iPhone.

Loud Speaker

You can usually tell if something's wrong with your loud speaker if audio is coated in a layer of static or other weird noises. Check and see if any dirt or dust is causing the problem and clean it out, if found. Didn't do the trick? Give us a heads up and we'll have it fixed in no time.
