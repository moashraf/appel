Making its mark on the world of smartphones in September 7, 2016, the iPhone 7 Plus hit the market touting a 5.5-inch screen, new color options, IP67 water and dust resistance, and much more. While on the outside, it resembles its smaller counterpart, the iPhone 7, the 7 Plus features a larger battery and bigger camera lens' two features not seen in its younger brother. And while, yes, it's true the phone is without a 7mm headphone jack, it replaces it with an advanced Taptic Engin that powers its new, non-physical home button.

The iPhone's top selling point remains its dual camera set-up. Two 12MP cameras, one of which is a wide-angle lens while the other a telephoto lens, allow for new picture-taking perks. These include both a 2x optical zoom (meaning no quality is lost when zooming in), better low-light performance, and a new Portrait Mode, which allows users to take artistic shots of subjects with clear foregrounds and blurred backgrounds.

iPhone 7 Plus Repairs and Screen Replacements

 

iPhone 7 Plus Screen Repair

That's a huge display on your 7 Plus. What a shame it'd be if that giant crack went unfixed. If you have a screen that's looking pretty much like a spiderweb, let us know. We'll send a repair technician to you for an iPhone 7 Plus screen replacement.

Charging Port

An iPhone without any juice won't do you much good at all. If your charging port isn't giving you a charge, check to see if dirt or dust might be blocking a connection, and clean, if necessary. That may not solve the issue, so give us a heads up. We'll fix it for you.

Microphone

If you've been talking on the phone and callers have been having significant trouble hearing you clearly due to fuzzy, crackling, or staticky noises, you may have dirt or lint stuck in your mic. See if that's the case and give it a clean. If that doesn't work, give us a holler and one of our all-star specialists will be on your way to fix your iPhone 7 Plus.

Ear Speaker

Hearing strange sounds when talking on the phone? Your ear speaker is probably not firing correctly. Inform our team and we'll schedule your on-demand repair appointment. Whenever, wherever you want, we can fix your iPhone.

Side Button

The side buttons on your 7 Plus are essential for conveniently adjusting your phone's volume. With them broken, doing that without going into your Settings app becomes a lot harder. Check if any dirt or grime is causing the buttons to be stuck. If cleaning it out doesn't work, contact our team and a member of our team will come to you and fix your iPhone 7 Plus.

Front/Rear Camera

That big, beautiful 12MP back camera is virtually useless if you can't take any photos with it. Are your pictures turning out black or blurry, or does your Camera app crash immediately upon opening it? You likely need your camera fixed.

Power Button

Dirt, grime, and other debris can typically be the cause behind a stuck power button. Give yours a look and a clean, if needed, and if that doesn't solve the problem, let us know. We'll get you set up with your on-demand iPhone 7 Plus repair.

Loud Speaker
