Introduced on March 31, 2016, the iPhone SE was positioned as a successor to the iPhone 5S with the upgraded hardware found in the iPhone 6S. While the SE kept the 5S's 4-inch screen and overall design, it's also packing the upgraded processor, rear camera, always-on Siri, Apple Pay, Live Photos, and tons of other features found in the 6S.

Because the iPhone SE's dimensions exactly match those of its predecessors, the iPhone 5 and 5S, all cases designed for those phones are able to fit it. At the time, this was additional good news to SE buyers. The first being the SE's form factor given the popularity of the 4-inch iPhone.

iPhone SE Repairs and Replacements

 

iPhone SE Screen Repairs

A broken screen on your iPhone can mean a lot of things. It could cause your display to not function as intended or, more commonly, it can just be a sore to look at. If your iPhone SE's suffering from a cracked screen, let us know. One of our iTechs will get you settled in with an iPhone SE screen repair.

Charging Port

There are a number of things that can be to blame for a broken charging port. Check and see if any dirt or dust has collected in yours, then clean, if necessary. If all's clear, then it may just be a hardware issue. Let us handle your iPhone SE repair.

Microphone

It can be incredibly irritating for your friends and family when they're on the phone with you and all they hear is strange, staticky noises or no sound at all. Check your mic to see if any dirt or other debris may have gotten trapped inside. If cleaning it doesn't work, you're probably in need of a replacement. Let us handle it for you.

Headphone Jack

With the iPhone, you have the power and convenience of all the media and entertainment in the world at your fingertips. Unfortunately, that doesn't mean much if your headphone jack is busted. If you're hearing weird noises or no sound at all when your ear buds or headphones are plugged in, check for any dirt or debris that may be causing interference. If you find nothing, let us know and we'll get your iPhone SE repair scheduled.

Home Button

Have you been pressing your home button over and over again to no avail? You could have dirt or grime underneath it causing it to stick. Confirm if that's the problem. If not, you're likely in need of a home button repair. Luckily for you, our of our all-star iTechs can handle it swiftly.

Ear Speaker

Are fuzzy, crackling, or staticky noises causing a disturbance on your phone calls, or are you having trouble hearing any sound at all? Don't worry: You aren't the first and definitely aren't the last to have this problem. Let us know by hitting "repair my iPhone SE" above and we'll get you set up with one of our repair technicians.

Side Button

Without the use of your side buttons, suddenly things like controlling your iPhone's volume become pretty difficult. See if dirt or grime are causing stickiness, then clean the area, if necessary. If that doesn't seem to be the problem, you might just need to get them fixed. Give us a heads up and we'll send one of our iTechs anywhere you want to fix your phone. 

Front/Rear Camera

Are your photos unexpectedly turning up blurry, or is your Camera app crashing on a regular basis? You could be in desperate need of a repair to your camera. We've mastered the art of iPhone camera repair long ago, just contact us and we'll get things squared away.

Power Button

Pressing your power button shouldn't require the strength of multiple people. If grime or other residue is causing your power button to be stuck, try giving your phone a cleaning. If that doesn't work, you may need your phone repaired. Let us know and we'll send a technician wherever, whenever you want to fix your iPhone SE.

Loud Speaker

You're looking at a loud speaker repair on your iPhone if you're hearing static or crackling sounds when playing audio out loud. If that sounds familiar, don't worry: We'll send an iTech straight to you at a location of your choosing, at a time of your choosing, to fix your phone in no time at all. How easy does that sound?
