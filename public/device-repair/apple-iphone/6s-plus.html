iPhone 6s Plus Repairs and Replacements

 

iPhone 6s Screen Repair

A screen as big as the 6s Plus's doesn't deserve a nasty scar running through it. If your iPhone's screen is cracked, give us a shout and we'll send out a tech to meet you.

Charging Port

Is your 6s Plus having difficulty keeping a charge? There might be dirt or dust obstructing your charging port. Give it a look and a clean - if that does not work, let us know and we can repair your iPhone 6s Plus.

Microphone

Without a working microphone, friends or family on the phone are going to have a pretty hard time hearing you clearly. If callers hear fuzzy, crackling noises, you may have dirt or any dirt stuck found in iPhone's mic. If cleaning it doesn't solve the problem, contact us.

Headphone Jack

Streaming services put millions of songs at your fingertips, but that does not do much good if you can't hear it. If you hear weird noises while listening to audio through your headphones, your headphone jack might either be dirty or in need of an iPhone 6s Plus repair. We can handle the latter.

Home Button

Are you running into issues pressing your home button? Check and see if dirt or grime could be causing it to stick, and then give it a clean, if needed. If the issue continues, inform us and we'll send a specialist to you to fix your phone.

Ear Speaker

A broken ear speaker is easily diagnosed through strange, crackling or staticky noises. If that sounds familiar, click "repair my iPhone 6s Plus" above and we'll set you up with a repair at a time and location of your choice.

Side Button

Adjusting the volume on your iPhone is made easy through the use of its side buttons. If one or all of them aren't working, check and see if maybe a build-up of dirt is making the buttons stick to the phone. If you're still running into issues, let us know and we'll sort things out.

Front/Rear Camera

The iPhone 6s Plus takes great photos, but you wouldn't be able to tell with a broken camera. Are your pictures showing up all blurred and maybe your camera apps aren't working properly? An issue with your phone's hardware could be at play. Contact us and we'll schedule an iPhone 6s Plus repair for you.

Power Button

A power button that's stuck means turning your iPhone on and off becomes much harder. If you're having difficulty pressing yours, check and see if any grime could be causing stickiness. If you find that isn't the issue, you might need a replacement. We'll solve that for you.

Loud Speaker

Fuzzy, crackling noises or little sound from your loud speaker usually means you're in need of a repair. Reach out to us and we'll send a member of our crack team of iTechs to a place of your choosing get your iPhone 6s Plus fixed.
