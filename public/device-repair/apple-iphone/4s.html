While it retained the outer appearance of its predecessor, the iPhone 4, Apple included a plethora of internal upgrades in the 4s, including an upgrade to the Apple A5 processor and an 8-megapixel camera capable of 1080p video recording.

Additionally, The iPhone 4s debuted the iOS 5 and launched with the iCloud, iMessage, Notification Center, Reminders and Twitter integration out of the box, the star of the show remained Siri. While past versions of iOS included a voice control system, the introduction of Siri allowed for the 4s to understand a wider variety of non-specific commands, even in natural language.

iPhone 4s Screen Replacements and Repairs

 

iPhone 4s Screen Replacement

Even a phone as old as the iPhone 4s doesn't deserve a cracked screen. If you have lines running through your display where there shouldnt be any, let us know and well get you scheduled for an iPhone 4s screen replacement.

Charging Port

An iPhone needs power to survive, and without a working charging port, that life force is denied. Before contacting us, check to see if any dirt or dust is blocking your charging port and clean it out, as necessary. 

Microphone

A phone is pretty useless if no one can hear you speak on it. If callers hear fuzzy or crackling noises when speaking to you, you might need to set yourself up with a microphone repair. No worries! We're masters at the art.

Headphone Jack

A 64GB iPhone 4s can hold around 13,000 songs, but they're all no good if theres a thick layer of fuzzy or crackling noises on top. Take a look and see if any dirt or dust in your jack is blocking a connection with your ear buds. If you find nothing, let us know and well get you set up with an iPhone 4s repair.

Home Button

A home button that refuses to take you home could be due to dirt or grime stuck beneath it. Take a look and see if any cleaning is required. If all's good, you're probably looking at needing a home button iPhone 4s repair.

Ear Speaker

Your iPhone's ear speaker serves a pretty important purpose: Allowing you to hear others when on calls. If you're hearing fuzzy or crackling noises, dirt, lint, dust, or other debris may be to blame. If you search and find none of the above, let us know and well get you set up with an iTech.

Side Button

Your iPhone's side buttons allow you to freely control its volume. Without them, you're forever doomed to the same volume level, unless you change it in your Settings app, but who wants to do that? Let us know if you need any of your side buttons fixed! we've got you covered.

Front/Rear Camera

The iPhone 4s introduced an 8-megapixel camera capable of shooting 1080p video, which won't do you any good if it's broken. If your camera hardware is on the fritz, let us know and we'll get everything squared away for you.

Power Button

Locking or turning off your iPhone 4s becomes a bit less convenient if your power button is busted. Check to see if dirt or dust is causing the problem and clean, if necessary. If your search turns up fruitless, turn to us, we'll send a repair expert to you to fix your phone.

Loud Speaker

YouTube videos and your massive music library won't sound too great played on your loud speaker if dirt or dust is stuck inside, instead, fuzzy or crackling sounds may rule supreme. Give it a good look-see and determine if cleaning it will solve your issue. If that fails, hit 'repair my iPhone 4s' above to get in contact with us.
