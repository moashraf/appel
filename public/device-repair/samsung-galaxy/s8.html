The Samsung Galaxy S8 (eight generation of Samsung's Galaxy S series) is a smartphone manufactured by Samsung Electronics. The S8 was released in North America on April 21st, 2017. Customers can choose to purchase the regular S8 or the phablet version, the S8+. 

How does the Samsung Galaxy S8 compare to the S7? Is the S8 the better buy? The display is the most significant change between generations. The S8 uses a 5.8-inch Super AMOLED display that covers 83% of the surface area of the front of the phone, while the S7 uses a 5.1-inch Super AMOLED that covers 72% of the surface area of the front of the phone. The Samsung Galaxy S8 runs with a lower resolution to optimize battery life. There is no physical home button on the S8, which means, you will have to use the fingerprint scanner next to the rear camera. The Samsung Galaxy S8 is dust and water resistant. The phone can stay submerged in 1.5 meters of water for a half-hour.

Despite its more substantial frame, larger screen size, and stronger glass, the eighth generation Galaxy S series smartphone is far from indestructible.
