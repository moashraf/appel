Samsung Note 4 Repairs and Replacements

 

Note 4 Screen Replacements

That big, beautiful screen on your Note 4 will look just plain ugly with a shattered display. If your phablet took a tumble down the stairs or a dive onto some hard cement, let us know and well take care of it.

Charging Port

How are you supposed to take advantage of the Note 4s fast charging without a working charging port? If yours isnt working, make sure there isnt any dirt or dust obstructing it. If that isnt the problem, you might be in need of a Samsung Note 4 repair.

Microphone

A phones no good if no one can hear you speak. Are callers having trouble hearing you speak clearly? Youre probably in desperate need of a mic Galaxy Note 4 repair. We can handle that.

Headphone Jack

You cant very well listen to music with static and crackling noises over it, can you? If youre hearing either of those things, you either have a dirty headphone jack or need the help of our iTechs.

Home Button

Is your home button not working or your fingerprint not scanning? Thats a big problem and might mean its time for a Galaxy Note 4 replacement.

Ear Speaker

A broken ear speaker can be a very irritating problem, especially if it means you either cant hear callers or instead hear tons of static or crackling noises. If this sounds familiar, quit worrying: Weve got it covered.

Side Button

Having trouble adjusting your Note 4s volume? Check and see if dirt or stickiness is the culprit. If cleaning your phone doesnt do the trick, let us know and well take care of it for you.

Front/Rear Camera

Are your photos turning up blurry or your camera apps crashing upon opening? If its the former, try cleaning your camera lens. If that doesnt work, or its the latter, you may be in need of a Samsung Note 4 repair.

Power Button

If your power button is stuck or refuses to work, its either dirty or due to a hardware problem. Try cleaning it, and if that doesnt help, give us a heads up. Well send one of our iTechs to you to fix it.

Loud Speaker

Watching videos without headphones on the Note 4s huge display can be pretty seriously hindered by a busted loud speaker. No one wants to hear static over their favorite media. Contact us now and get it repaired.
