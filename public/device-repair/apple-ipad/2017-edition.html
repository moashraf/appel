iPad 2017 Edition Repairs and Replacements

 

iPad Screen Repair

The large 9.7-inch glass screen is prone to shattering as a result of an accidental drop. Our techs can perform an iPad screen repair to make your device look brand new. 

Charging Port

Your Apple iPad has a versatile connection port on the bottom. It's used for transferring data to and from iTunes and lets you charge up the battery whenever you're running out of juice. If it's not working due to poor connection or damaged wiring, you're going to have a hard time charging your tablet. Give us a call when it stops working for a guaranteed repair. 

Microphone

The iPad's microphone is seamlessly integrated into its body. Whether it's damaged from dust or water, contact us today for a fix. We'll make sure that friends and family on the other side of your FaceTime chat can hear you clearly. 

Headphone Jack

Without a working headphone jack, you won't be able to enjoy your apps and media in private. If it produces a loud crackling noise or has stopped working altogether, let our Apple iPad repair technicians take a look and fix the problem for you. 

Home Button

The home button with Touch ID sensor is at the heart of your device. If you're having trouble gaining access or making payments with Apple Pay, give us a call for a new home button. 

Ear Speaker

The ear speaker gives you the crisp and clear audio you need to enjoy FaceTime conversations, movies, and music. Unfortunately, the speaker on your Apple iPad can become damaged over time due to an accumulation of dirt or faulty wiring. If this happens, we'll take care of it. 

Side Button

The iPad's side buttons are crucial for controlling the audio levels of your media at a moment's notice. If dust, debris, or physical damage is preventing you from doing so, our iPad repair professionals can come and fix the problem in no time. 

Front/Rear Camera

The impressive clarity and HD video capabilities of the Apple iPad are rendered useless if you don't have a functioning camera. If you're experiencing a cracked lens, blurry photos, or the inability to open the camera application at all, let us take a look and provide you with an efficient fix. 

Power Button

Located at the top of your device, the power button provides you with multiple functions. Not only does it turn the iPad on or off, but it's also used to wake it up. Let us provide you with repair services to get your power button in good working condition again.

Loud Speaker

You can't enjoy all that the Apple iPad has to offer if you can't hear it. Are you relegated to listening on your headphones because of unwanted distortion from your speakers? Let the expert repair technicians at iCracked come up with a repair solution that works.
