The iPad Mini 4 (fourth generation of Apple's tablet computer) was released in America on September 9, 2015. Both the Wifi and 4G models are available for purchase. This tablet was announced following the "Hey Siri" special event. With the announcement of the Apple iPad Mini, 4 came added support for 802.11ac Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 4.2.

The iPad Mini 4 comes with an extra gigabyte of RAM compared to previous generations of the Apple iPad, split view multitasking options, a wider body, and a thinner design. The fourth-generation iPad Mini is 33.2 grams lighter than the previous generation iPad. The iPad Mini four ditched the tri-core A8X for a faster, lighter dual-core A8 processor. The newer processor was added to improve multitasking capabilities. This talent comes standard with the same depth measurement (6.1 millimeters) as the second generation iPad.

Despite its wider body, new multitasking functions, and faster processor, the fourth generation iPad is not damage-proof.
